Introduction

TracePlay ABC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children under the age of 13 and complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). As with all our educational apps, we prioritize children's privacy and safety above all else. This privacy policy explains our information practices regarding children's privacy.

Information Collection

What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information from children or adults, including:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics
  • Analytics data
  • Behavioral tracking
  • Contact information
  • Photos or videos
  • Voice recordings (beyond temporary device memory for letter pronunciation)
  • In-app messages or chat features

App Functionality

Our app:

  • Works completely offline
  • Stores no data outside the device
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Has no third-party integrations
  • Contains no advertisements
  • Has no in-app purchases
  • Includes no external links
  • Has no social features

Technical Operation

  • All app content is self-contained within the installation package
  • Audio playback for letter pronunciation uses device-level functionality only
  • Temporary drawing data is stored only in device memory and is cleared when exiting the app
  • No data persistence between sessions

Data Security

Since we don't collect any personal information:

  • No data is stored on servers
  • No data is transmitted over networks
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • No data retention policies needed

Parental Rights

Parents can:

  • Review our privacy practices at any time through this policy
  • Contact us with questions about privacy
  • Be assured that no personal information is collected about their children

Educational Content

Like our other educational app Math Mind Sharpener, TracePlay ABC provides:

  • Age-appropriate educational content
  • Safe, contained learning environment
  • No external web browsing capabilities
  • No social media integration
  • No user-generated content

Third-Party Services

We do NOT use any third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • User tracking
  • Content delivery
  • Social media
  • Data collection

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make any material changes to this privacy policy, we will:

  • Post the new policy here
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users through the App Store update notes
  • Require explicit acceptance of any changes that affect data collection
