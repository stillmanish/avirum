Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • App Store account information for subscription management
  • Transaction and receipt data
  • Usage analytics to improve our services
  • iCloud data for syncing your ledger entries
  • Email data (if Gmail integration is enabled)
  • Receipt images and their extracted data

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process your transactions and manage subscriptions
  • To improve our AI and machine learning features
  • To analyze app usage and enhance user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important updates about our services

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your ledger data is stored securely in iCloud
  • Local data is stored on your device
  • Backups are encrypted
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Your Rights and Control

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Delete all your data using the 'Delete All My Data' option in Settings

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple's StoreKit for subscription management
  • AI services for receipt processing
  • Analytics services for app improvement
  • iCloud for data syncing
  • Gmail API (optional) for transaction import

6. Use of Google User Data

  • Data obtained from Google APIs, including Gmail, is used solely to provide features within the Ledgify app, such as importing receipts and transactions at the user's request.
  • We do not use data obtained from Google APIs to develop, improve, or train generalized artificial intelligence (AI) or machine learning (ML) models.
  • Google user data is not shared with third parties except as necessary to provide the requested app functionality, and is never used for model training or improvement.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • We retain your data as long as you use our services.
  • All your data is deleted immediately upon your request for account deletion or when you use the 'Delete All My Data' option in Settings.
  • We do not retain backup data after deletion.

8. Analytics and Your Choices

  • Analytics are only collected if you enable them in the app.
  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone.
  • You can opt out of analytics at any time in the app settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
