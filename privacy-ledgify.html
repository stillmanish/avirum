Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • App Store account information for subscription management
  • Transaction and receipt data
  • Usage analytics to improve our services
  • iCloud data for syncing your ledger entries

2. Subscription Data

  • We process subscription transactions through Apple's App Store
  • Your payment information is handled securely by Apple
  • We do not store your payment details
  • Subscription status is used to provide access to premium features

3. Data Usage

  • Your data is used to provide and improve our services
  • We use AI to process receipts and transactions
  • Analytics help us understand app usage and improve features
  • We do not sell your personal information

4. Data Storage

  • Your ledger data is stored securely in iCloud
  • Local data is stored on your device
  • Backups are encrypted
  • You can delete all your data at any time using the 'Delete All My Data' option in Settings, which permanently removes all local and iCloud data

5. Third-Party Services

  • We use Apple's StoreKit for subscriptions
  • AI services process receipt data
  • Analytics services help improve the app
  • All third-party services comply with privacy laws
