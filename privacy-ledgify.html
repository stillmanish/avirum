Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • App Store account information for subscription management
  • Transaction and receipt data
  • Usage analytics to improve our services
  • iCloud data for syncing your ledger entries
  • Email data (if Gmail integration is enabled)
  • Receipt images and their extracted data

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process your transactions and manage subscriptions
  • To improve our AI and machine learning features
  • To analyze app usage and enhance user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important updates about our services

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your ledger data is stored securely in iCloud
  • Local data is stored on your device
  • Backups are encrypted
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Your Rights and Control

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Delete all your data using the 'Delete All My Data' option in Settings

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple's StoreKit for subscription management
  • AI services for receipt processing
  • Analytics services for app improvement
  • iCloud for data syncing
  • Gmail API (optional) for transaction import

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your data as long as you use our services
  • Data is deleted when you request account deletion
  • Backup data is retained for 30 days after deletion
  • Analytics data is anonymized after 12 months

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
