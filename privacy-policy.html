By accessing or using the App, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with our App, such as the conversations you have with historical figures, features you use, and time spent in the App.

Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

We use the information we collect to:
